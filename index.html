Hiking

We’ve always loved to hike together, from Marin to Big Sur. For a short hike in Saratoga or Los Gatos, Montalvo and the Los Gatos Creek Trail are two great options. A bit further afield, we love hiking at Bear Creek Redwoods, Castle Rock, and Wunderlich. If you’d like to explore the broader Bay Area, Mt. Tamalpais, Angel Island, and Point Tomales are all beautiful.

Wineries & Breweries

On the weekends, we often enjoy visiting breweries and wineries around the Bay Area. Some of our favorites are Barebottle in San Francisco and Santa Clara, Fieldwork’s taproom in Hillsdale, and Redwood Coast Cider in San Carlos. While you’re here, we recommend trying wine from the Santa Cruz Mountains AVA. Picchetti Winery, Ridge Winery, and Left Bend Winery are a few of our favorites.

San Francisco

We’ve called San Francisco home for the last five years. On a sunny day, hike through the Presidio or Lands End and get amazing ocean views, bike across the Golden Gate Bridge to Sausalito, or soak up the sun (and enjoy the people watching) at Dolores Park. Some of our favorite cultural and historic sights include the Legion of Honor, Palace of Fine Arts, and Haight Ashbury. Don’t forget to try a classic Mission burrito before you leave!

The Beach

We are just a short 30-minute drive from the beaches of Santa Cruz. Our perfect day at the beach starts with Verve Coffee, followed by a walk around the cute beach town of Capitola. From there, enjoy lunch at Pizza My Heart, check out the Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk, and stop by the Penny Ice Creamery. If you’re brave, you can take a dip in the cold Pacific Ocean!
